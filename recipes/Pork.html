Marinated Grilled Pork Tenderloin


Description:

This marinated pork loin cooks up nicely on the grill. It tastes best when marinated for 24 hours. Great for summer barbecues and family friendly gatherings.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 4 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Pork- 2 (12 ounce) pork tenderloins.

Directions:

  1. Make marinade: Whisk together honey, soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, ginger, ketchup, garlic, parsley, onion powder, cayenne pepper, and cinnamon in a medium bowl; pour into a resealable plastic bag.

  2. Place pork tenderloins into the bag coat and marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour or up to 24 hours.

  3. Preheat the grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.

  4. Remove pork tenderloins from marinade; shake off excess. Discard remaining marinade.

  5. Cook pork tenderloins on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center, 20-30 minutes, turning occasionally. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the centers should read at least 145 degree F (63 degrees C).
