Italian Chicken Marinade


Description:

The Italian Chicken Marinade is a simple recipe that adds a special flavor before grilling. This recipe only takes 15 minutes to prep.

This recipe involves whisking the ingredients together and marinating them for 4 hours before grilling them. Additionally bring thermometers to ensure proper temperature is reached.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Directions:

  1. Whisk salad dressing, garlic powder, and salt together; add chicken breasts and turn to coat. Cover dish with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator, 4 hours to overnight.

  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate

  3. Remove chicken from marinade and shake off excess discard remaining marinade.

  4. Cook chicken on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, about 7-8 minutes on each side. For further instruction- an instant read thermometer inserted into the center of the meat should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
